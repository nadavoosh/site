Eleventhour

Our clock divides the day into two 12-hour halves.

Obviously, 12 was a fundamental number for many ancient civilizations, which gave us our 12 hour days and nights.

I wondered what our clocks would look like if that number was different. What if we had 11 hour days? or 24 hour days? Or 37 hour days? With 11 hours, the differences are subtle but drive the clock into uncanny-valley territory, which is territory I like exploring.

You can generate a clock with any arbitrary number of hours by changing the number after the hash sign here. The default is 11.
